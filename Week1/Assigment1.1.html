Ingredients (240 ml cup used)

  • Half cup fresh coconut grated or chopped
  • 2 tbsp fried gram/roasted chana dal
  • Half tbsp jeera/cumin seeds
  • 1 to 2 green chillies
  • Salt as needed
  • 1 garlic clove (optional or replace with small piece of ginger)

Seasoning

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Half tsp mustard
  • Half tsp Urad Dal
  • Pinch of hing
  • 1 spring curry leaves
  • 1 broken red chilli

How to make the recipe

  1. Blend together coconut, dal,garlic,salt,chilli and cumin well
  2. Add water just enough to blend and make a thick chutney.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and cumin, red chilli and dal
  4. When the dal turns golden, add curry leaves & hing.
  5. Add it to chutney.
