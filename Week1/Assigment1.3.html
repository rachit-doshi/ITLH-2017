FEDERAL MANAGERS ASSOCIATION (FMA) OF YUMA

CHAPTER 247

Anually the local FMA Chapter 247 recognizes graduating High School Seniors by awarding two or three $500.00 scholarships.

Eligibility: High School Senior with a GPA of 3.0 or higher who will be attending college and is interested in a public service career. US Citizenship required.

SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS

Submit the following items to: FMA Chapter 247, Attn: Mrs. Mry Ellen Finch, Secretary, PO BOX 12671, YUMA AZ 85366. Deadline: Must be received by 1 April of each year.

  1. Cover Page - Include the name of the scholarship and the applicant's name. The student's high school name and/or logo are optional.
  2. Attach the following required documents:
  1. FMA Chapter #247 Application Form - application form must be typed
  2. A typed Personal Resume
  3. A typed essay no more than two pages describing your interest in a public service career.
  4. An official academic transcript- A current transcript can be obtained from the registrar.
  5. Copy of letter of acceptnce to college if available
  6. This instructional sheet signed
